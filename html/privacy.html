Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NeuroCare AI. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and health-related information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our application.

Note: NeuroCare AI is currently a demonstration platform. All data is stored locally in your browser leveraging localStorage. We do not upload, process, or store your personal data on external servers.

2. Information We Collect

While using the NeuroCare AI platform, you may provide the following information which is kept strictly on your local device:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, and demographic details.
  • Health and Developmental Data: M-CHAT screening results, developmental milestones, clinical notes, and behavioral observations.
  • Media Files: Video uploads for behavioral analysis (kept locally).

3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used to:

  • Generate personalized AI screening reports and risk assessments.
  • Maintain your child's developmental timeline and progress tracking charts.
  • Facilitate the scheduling of clinical appointments and follow-ups.
  • Render the correct, role-based dashboard interface (Parent vs. Clinician).

4. Data Storage and Security

Since this is a client-side web application, your data remains entirely within the confines of the browser you are using. Clearing your browser's local storage or cache will permanently delete your personalized NeuroCare AI data.

If this platform transitions to a cloud-based infrastructure in the future, it will be designed with end-to-end encryption, strict access controls, and full compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over the data entered into NeuroCare AI. You can delete your account and all associated patient records from your settings page, which will immediately clear the relevant localStorage entries.
