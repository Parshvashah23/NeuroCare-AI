The Process

Three steps to early insight

No hospital visit required. Start from home with just a phone camera and 5 minutes of your time.

01
📋

Behavioral Questionnaire

Answer 10 simple yes/no questions about your child's behavior, communication, and social patterns. Based on M-CHAT screening principles.

02
🎥

Video Analysis

Upload a short 30-second video of your child. Our AI analyzes facial attention, eye contact patterns, and movement characteristics.

03
📊

AI Risk Report

Receive an instant, explainable risk score with detailed breakdown and personalized next-step recommendations for parents and clinicians.

04
👩‍⚕️

Clinician Review

Share your AI-generated report directly with your pediatrician. Full clinical dashboard available for healthcare providers.
